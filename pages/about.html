Senior Interface Designer

Bradley Hawkins is a Pennsylvania based senior interface designer working full time at Andculture. He is currently focused on designing digital products to improve the future of online education. For past two semesters he taught an Interactive Graphic Design course as an adjunct professor at Susquehanna University. When not designing he enjoys spending time with his family, hiking, riding bmx, and traveling when possible. Download his resume.
