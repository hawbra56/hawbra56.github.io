Trytn started as an idea to create a mobile diving app after lots of research the user experience decided a better course of action was to create an ecommerce storefront plateform targeted at small boating and diving outfits. I was responsible for collaborating with the UX team, creating an identity, and designing the online product. Due to scheduling conflicts I switched to an Art Director role as the design for the product changed hands towards the end of the project to Madeline Pellman.
