Do you ever feel like you’re the only one who doesn’t have it all together, but it seems like everyone else DOES?

When this happens, you do what makes sense at the time - you put a smile on your face and you get busy doing what you’re supposed to do. Day in and day out. Before you know it, the joy you once had is gone, so much of what you do feels like a chore, and the smile on your face is so fake even you don’t recognize yourself.

Sound familiar? Ring any bells?

“Performing” your way through the day is not what God intended when He sent Jesus to give His children abundant life. Yes, of course we need to simply obey God regardless of how we feel, but what we’re talking about here goes much deeper than that. Practiced long enough, the “performance” becomes just that – a performance. Life’s not real to you anymore and you’re just functioning and acting your way through the day. Deep down, you know your heart has grown farther and farther away from God and you’re not honoring Him when you’re living like this. You desperately want to change, but you’re not quite sure how, or even where, to begin.

So what do you do?

Oh, Girlfriend, please join us for our 4th conference tour of Girlfriend Getaway – GFG 2.0!

In one great Saturday, you will be filled up with 3 praise and worship and teaching sessions on this very topic, where we pray you will be refreshed and restored in the Lord. You will see that you’re not the only one who struggles, and you’ll leave with a better understanding of the problem so many of us face. We’ll talk about why it’s so easy to fall into the performance trap and how we can get out of it and live a joyful, real, abundant life with God’s help.

Bring some of your Girlfriends, ok? Chances are pretty great that they struggle just like you do and could use a day of refreshment too! And of course, every GFG offers great fellowship, food, and fun – because EVERY woman needs a break, right?

Be sure to check out our video and other pages on our site carefully - we’ve made some big changes to Girlfriend Getaway GFG 2.0 and are excited to see you at an upcoming conference!
