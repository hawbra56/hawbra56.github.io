Key conference verse:

“They celebrate your abundant goodness and joyfully sing of your righteousness.” ~ Psalm 145:7 (NIV)

Life is hard? Indeed it can be. Struggles are part of life? Yep, they most certainly are. Everyone needs to find the way through it all somehow? Yes, we do – and we can!

Regardless of how hard life is and despite the struggles it presents, God is faithful - He has already provided the way through! It’s so important to remember that He is so very good; He is still in control; and He is worthy to be praised for all of the above!

In 2009, God laid the ministry of Girlfriend Getaway on Cindy Foor’s heart with a focus to reach and teach women of all ages and seasons of life the Word of Truth. And for the next 6 years, she and her team strived to seek and follow Him as He faithfully guided them through one tour after another, year after year. Through each conference women were refreshed and lives were changed, just as God promises -

“I will refresh the weary and satisfy the faint.” ~ Jeremiah 31:25 (NIV)

This year Cindy senses God guiding her to conclude Girlfriend Getaway with one more tour – at least for now and until God leads otherwise. In the Bible the number 7 is a symbol of completion, so for our 7th tour we’re going to throw a “wrap-up party” for God to celebrate His faithfulness – and YOU’RE INVITED!

It will be a joyful celebration as we take a look back at our first 6 tours and give God the glory and praise for all He has done for us and in us through His Son, Jesus Christ. Without question, there is much to be celebrated and God deserves this party thrown in His honor! Whether you’ve attended every Girlfriend Getaway conference, missed one here or there, or haven’t attended one yet – you don’t want to miss this one, Girlfriend! Just like our other tours, you’ll enjoy one wonderful day with three sessions of worship and biblical teaching, and lots of great fellowship, food, and fun!

As God is lifted up and celebrated, you will be encouraged and refreshed knowing that you’re a child of the King of all Kings! Bring some Girlfriends and join us as we CELEBRATE Him!
