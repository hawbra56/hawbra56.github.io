Key conference verse:

"Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind and with all your strength. The second is this: Love your neighbor as yourself. There is no greater commandment than these." Jesus ~ Mark 12:30-31

Have you ever seen an old washing machine when it’s out of balance and it appears as though it’s going to walk itself right out of the laundry room? It’s scary, right?

Have you ever felt like that old washing machine? When life seems so far out of balance that YOU would like to walk yourself out - even for a little while? This can be even more daunting than that old monster in your laundry room!

No matter how hard you try, you just can’t seem to get it all together - at least for very long. Some areas of life are going well but others are in total disarray. Everything you’re trying to juggle just isn’t staying in the air long enough. There’s just too much to manage, so you’re struggling, and consequently life is way out of balance.

Without proper balance, you will stagger. You will lose your sense of direction. You will drop things. You will struggle to maintain your grip. You will grow weary and eventually, you will be exhausted. You will fall. You will feel like you’re a full-blown failure because you just can’t seem to keep everything in sync.

So, what’s a girl to do?

Well, dear one, please be encouraged, because at one point or another, on one day or another, during one season of life or another, every woman has felt like you do. You must join us for our 5th Girlfriend Getaway tour entitled “First Things First: Becoming Better Balanced,” where we will delve into God’s Word to discover His timely answers to the dilemma we all face.

To restore proper balance, that old washing machine needs to be realigned, reloaded, and restarted, Girlfriend. And sometimes, so do we!
