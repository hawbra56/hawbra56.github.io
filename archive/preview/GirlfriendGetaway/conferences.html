Aren’t you tired of hearing this lie? Don’t you want to stop giving it power in your life? Do you want to learn how to destroy it, rather than let it destroy you?

Join us for our next Girlfriend Getaway conference series and learn how to attack the lie that trips up every woman at one point or another. Learn how to say “Enough is Enough,” how to destroy the lie, and how to grab onto the truth that, in Jesus, you ARE enough.

Come, Girlfriend! Take great delight in the truth that your loving, faithful, all-powerful God is always MORE than enough. He’s so eager to prove that to you!
