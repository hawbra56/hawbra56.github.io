Game Development

Little Bird Games makes educational and therapeutic video, board, and card games. We offer full service serious game development based on the most current research and best practices from the fields of instructional design and game design.

Teaching

For kids
We offer customized half-day events, great for children and young adults, with activities that teach basic game design techniques. Events can be tailored to address specific topics or to utilize resources unique to your location.

For educators
Our team can deliver professional development sessions that cover a wide range of topics. This includes, but is not limited to, strategies for using games and game design tools in the classroom.

For businesses
We offer courses on educational game development for corporate training. This is a great option for organizations that want to explore internal game design and development but lack the expertise to get started.
